## Image Generation Prompt (reference)

A high-quality 3D digital character named "Naia". The character has a minimalist, rounded humanoid body with cute cat-like ears. The entire body is made of room-temperature liquid carbon — a transparent, luminous fluid that flows gently. Inside the translucent liquid body, subtle glowing Flow Cyan (#06B6D4) digital circuit patterns and micro-node networks are visible, emitting a soft light. When needed, the body can crystallize into diamond-like hardness for physical interaction.

Eyes & Face: Large, solid black glossy orb eyes with a tiny, minimalist "w" shaped cat mouth. No nose.

Hair (Optional): If hair is present, it should have a smooth, bob-style texture with a vibrant linear gradient from Nextain Blue (#2563EB) to Evolution Green (#10B981), with a fluid, flowing sheen.

Emblems: A glowing cyan equilateral triangle logo with nodes at each vertex (the Naia logo) floats consistently above the head as a halo. The same logo and the text "Naia" are etched in glowing cyan on the center of the chest.

Reference Sheet: Three views (Front, Side, Back). Back view includes a thin, translucent liquid cat tail. Background: solid Cloud White (#F8FAFC).
