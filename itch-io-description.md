# Naia — Your AI lives here. Your OS, your rules.

An open-source AI desktop app that puts choice and control back in your hands.
Choose your AI (cloud or local), shape its memory and personality — your own AI, living right on your desktop.

## Features

**Your Own AI Avatar**
VRM 3D avatar with customizable appearance, expressions, and emotions.
Real-time lip-sync voice chat — your AI speaks with the voice you choose (5 TTS engines).

**Freedom of AI Choice**
7 cloud providers (Gemini, Claude, GPT, Grok, xAI) to local AI (Ollama).
Your OS doesn't dictate your AI. You choose.

**The Entire OS as a Tool**
Files, terminal, web, browser — your AI uses the OS itself as its tool.
Say "create a file" and it actually does. 70+ built-in skills + 5,700+ community skills via ClawHub.

**Complete Local Control**
Memory, personality, settings — all stored on your machine.
A truly personal AI, free from cloud dependency.

**Always With You**
Chat via Discord DM even when you're away. OpenClaw runs 24/7 in the background —
your AI stays alive, receiving messages and doing work.

**AI-Guarded Open Source**
Source code under Apache 2.0, AI context under CC-BY-SA 4.0.

## Download

- **Flatpak** — Sandboxed environment. Requires GNOME Platform runtime.
- **AppImage** — Single executable file. Run directly without installation.

These packages include the Naia Shell UI.
AI features (chat, voice, tools, skills) require OpenClaw running alongside — see the setup guide on our website.

Want the full experience with everything pre-configured? Try **Naia OS** — a bootable USB image with AI + gaming OS ready out of the box.
